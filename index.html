Short Bio: Mengying Zhao received the BE degree from the School of Computer Science and Technology, Shandong University, China, in July 2011, and the PhD degree from the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong, in July 2015. She is now a professor with the School of Computer Science and Technology, Shandong University. Her research interests include computer architecture, non-volatile memory, and heterogeneous computing.
